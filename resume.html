General Résumé Template

Project Overview

Created a general résumé template with consistent formatting tailored for CS majors in the US, Canada, and EU. It contains some general advice for first-time writing a résumé or editing an existing one.

I created this template and this page because I helped many people for free, and it is a tremendous effort to repeat every piece of advice. So, I will be adding frequent résumé mistakes.

Disclaimer

"No 'one size fits all'"; This template is unintended to be used 'as is' but as a layout. Layout varies from country to country and role to role. This principle also applies to the advice here. I still did not add all the advice, and not all the advice applies to everyone. You should consult a résumé writer for style and proofreading, or at least an English-native friend for proofreading.

I am a résumé writer, and I provide free feedback and tips. However, I am not a résumé reviewer. The difference is that I fix style and proofread (vocab and grammar mistakes), but I don't tell you how to tailor your résumé for a specific position or company. However, I have unofficially reviewed a Software Architect résumé in Fortune 100, Senior Tester for EU. 30+ Inters and Entry Level résumés for FAANG+.

Finally, If I did help you, it helps me a lot of you recommend my résumé writing on LinkedIn.

Common Mistakes

1. Never 100% depend on AI. It exaggerates and makes grammatical mistakes. Test it yourself and ask natives.
2. Your résumé might appear flawless in your eyes if you haven't seen enough résumés, just like chess. If you don't play chess, all you see is the pieces, not the pattern between them.
3. If you can't find a résumé writer, have multiple people look at your résumé.
